10 High-Paying Freelance Skills You Can Learn in 30 Days

Published: November 10, 2025

The freelance economy is booming, and you don't need years of experience to get started. Here are skills that can quickly turn into profitable income streams:

Beginner's Guide to Dividend Investing: Earn Passive Income Monthly

Published: November 7, 2025

Dividend investing is one of the most reliable ways to build passive income. Here's how to start with as little as R20:

  • Dividend ETFs: Instant diversification (SCHD, VYM, VIG)
  • REITs: Real estate income without buying property
  • Blue Chip Stocks: Established companies with consistent payouts
  • Dividend Aristocrats: Companies increasing dividends for 25+ years

Start with a brokerage account (Fidelity, Schwab, or Robinhood), invest regularly, and reinvest dividends. A R150 portfolio yielding 4% generates R40,000/year in passive income.
